What is Water Tracker?

We are a water tracking application and our mission is to impart a greater knowledge of Hydration leading to Health and Happiness for the world.

opacity
Why did we make this app?

Water Tracker was made to promote health and teach our community about the amount of water one would need to drink in order to maintain health and happiness.

opacity
What does it allow the user to do?

Water Tracker allows the user to track the amount of water they drink in a day and update them on how much more they need.
